Things to Know About Pirates of the Caribbean

  1. Jack Sparrow is the original owner of the Black Pearl.
    • It was originally named Queen Anne's Revenge.
    • Barbossa stole it from Jack before the plot of POTC 1.
  2. William Turner has the blood of a true pirate.
    • William's father, Bill Turner, was a pirate that Jack knew personally.
    • Due to his father's true character towards piracy, the blood was passed to Will.
    • Will's bloodline made him the main focus of Barbossa's mission.
  3. Jack is directionless and is often unsure of what he wants.
    • This is shown through clips of Jack looking at his compass-- it occasionally spinning without a goal.
    • Jack's uncertainty reflects the ironic sense of humanity within his pirate mindset.
  4. Elizabeth Swann is annoying.
    • She had at LEAST three love interests.
      • Jack Sparrow
      • William Turner
      • James Norrington
    • Her voice is annoying and she is extremely entitled-- as expected from the Governor's Daughter.
